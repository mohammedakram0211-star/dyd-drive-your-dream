The process

Four steps to your dream ride

From booking to the open road, every ride follows the same disciplined sequence — built for safety first, thrill second.

01 / Book

Pick your bike

Choose the model and package online or at our RK Beach kiosk. Reserve a slot in under a minute.

02 / Verify

Gear up

Show your license, sign the waiver, leave a refundable deposit. We fit your helmet and riding gear.

03 / Ride

Hit the coast

Follow our guide along a fixed beach-road route. GPS-tracked, escort-supported, zero free-roaming risk.

04 / Decide

Know your bike

Walk away with photos, a real feel for the machine, and — if you're in — a warm intro to the dealer.

Built on trust

Safety isn't
an add-on. It's
the product.

A ₹4–15 lakh machine and a first-time rider is a serious responsibility. Every safeguard below runs on every single ride, no exceptions.

RIDER

License & age verified

Valid license and minimum age 21, checked on the spot before any bike is handed over.

RIDER

Security deposit

A refundable deposit is held for the duration of the ride and returned after inspection.

ROUTE

Guided, not free-roam

Every ride follows a fixed coastal route with an escort — never an open hand-off.

ROUTE

Live GPS tracking

Every bike is tracked in real time for the full duration of the experience.

BIKE

Pre & post-ride inspection

Photo and video condition checks happen before you ride and the moment you return.

BIKE

Insured & agreement-backed

Every ride is covered by a signed liability waiver and appropriate insurance arrangement.
